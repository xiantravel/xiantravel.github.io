Xian’s Top Rooftop Bars with Stunning Views

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Xian, an ancient city steeped in history, is also home to a vibrant nightlife scene. While the terracotta warriors and ancient city walls draw millions of visitors, the city’s rooftop bars offer a modern contrast with breathtaking views of its skyline. Whether you’re sipping cocktails under the stars or enjoying a sunset over the Bell Tower, these rooftop spots are must-visits for travelers looking to unwind in style.

The Best Rooftop Bars in Xian

1. Cloud 9 Rooftop Lounge

Perched atop one of Xian’s tallest buildings, Cloud 9 offers panoramic views of the city, including the iconic Giant Wild Goose Pagoda. The sleek, modern design and ambient lighting create a sophisticated atmosphere perfect for a night out.

Why Visit?
- Signature cocktails inspired by local flavors
- Live DJ sets on weekends
- Unmatched views of the pagoda illuminated at night

2. The Sky Garden at Gran Meliá Xian

This luxurious rooftop bar combines elegance with nature, featuring lush greenery and a serene poolside setting. The bar overlooks the Qujiang Lake, making it an ideal spot for a romantic evening.

Highlights:
- Crafted mixology with premium spirits
- A curated selection of tapas and light bites
- Stunning sunset views over the lake

3. Horizon Rooftop Bar

Located near the Bell Tower, Horizon offers a prime vantage point to admire Xian’s historic center. The bar’s contemporary design contrasts beautifully with the ancient architecture below.

Don’t Miss:
- Their famous "Terracotta Sour" cocktail
- Happy hour deals from 5-7 PM
- Cozy fire pits for cooler evenings

Unique Experiences at Xian’s Rooftop Bars

Rooftop Dining with a View

Many of Xian’s rooftop bars also serve gourmet cuisine. At The Moonlight Terrace, you can enjoy a fusion of Shaanxi and Western dishes while gazing at the city lights. Their lamb skewers with a modern twist are a crowd favorite.

Themed Nights and Events

From jazz nights to cultural performances, rooftop bars like Nebula Lounge host regular events that blend entertainment with stunning backdrops. Check their schedules for special themed evenings.

Photography Hotspots

For Instagram-worthy shots, head to Starlit Rooftop, where the neon lights of Xian’s skyline create a mesmerizing backdrop. Their infinity-edge seating area is perfect for capturing the perfect travel moment.

Tips for Visiting Xian’s Rooftop Bars

  • Timing Matters: Arrive before sunset to secure the best seats.
  • Dress Code: Some upscale bars enforce a smart-casual dress policy.
  • Reservations: Popular spots like Cloud 9 often require advance booking, especially on weekends.

Whether you’re a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, Xian’s rooftop bars offer unforgettable experiences high above the ancient city. So grab a drink, soak in the views, and toast to the perfect night in Xian.
