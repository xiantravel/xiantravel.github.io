Hostels in Xian with Free Pub Crawls & Bar Hops

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Xi’an, the ancient capital of China, is not just about terracotta warriors and historic city walls. It’s also a vibrant city with a growing nightlife scene, especially for backpackers and budget travelers. If you’re looking for a fun, social, and affordable way to experience Xi’an after dark, staying at a hostel that offers free pub crawls and bar hops is a game-changer. These hostels not only provide cheap accommodation but also organize epic nights out, helping you meet fellow travelers while exploring the best bars and clubs in town.

Why Choose a Hostel with Free Pub Crawls in Xi’an?

Traveling on a budget doesn’t mean missing out on the fun. Hostels that include free pub crawls take the hassle out of planning your nightlife adventures. Here’s why they’re worth it:

  • Meet Like-Minded Travelers – Pub crawls are the perfect icebreaker. You’ll bond with other backpackers over drinks and shared experiences.
  • Local Insights – Hostel staff know the best spots—hidden bars, cheap drink deals, and the liveliest clubs.
  • Safety in Numbers – Exploring nightlife in a foreign city can be intimidating. Joining a group makes it safer and more enjoyable.
  • Free or Discounted Drinks – Many pub crawls include welcome shots or drink specials at partner bars.

Top Hostels in Xi’an Offering Free Pub Crawls

1. The Hangout Hostel Xi’an

Located near the Bell Tower, this hostel is a favorite among solo travelers. Their weekly pub crawl takes guests to three different bars, ending at a popular club in the city center.

Highlights:
- Free welcome drink at the first bar
- Discounted cocktails at select venues
- A mix of expat-friendly bars and local hotspots

2. Xi’an Qixian Youth Hostel

This cozy hostel in the Muslim Quarter not only offers a great location but also organizes bar-hopping nights twice a week.

Why Stay Here?
- Walking distance to street food stalls (perfect for pre-drink snacks)
- Themed party nights (e.g., “80s Retro Night”)
- Friendly staff who join the crawl

3. Shuyuan International Youth Hostel

For those who love a mix of history and nightlife, this hostel is inside a traditional courtyard building. Their free bar tours introduce guests to Xi’an’s emerging craft beer scene.

Perks:
- Visits to hidden speakeasies
- Beer-tasting sessions
- Late-night dumpling runs included

What to Expect on a Xi’an Pub Crawl

Every hostel’s pub crawl is different, but here’s a general idea of how these nights unfold:

Pre-Game at the Hostel

Most crawls start with a meet-and-greet at the hostel bar. This is where you’ll get to know your group and maybe enjoy a cheap happy-hour beer.

Bar #1: The Warm-Up Spot

Usually a laid-back bar or pub where you can grab a drink and chat. Some places offer free shots for crawl participants.

Bar #2: The Party Gets Louder

The second stop is often a livelier venue—maybe a cocktail bar or a place with live music.

Final Stop: The Club

The night typically ends at a dance club where you can party until the early hours. Some hostels even negotiate free entry for their groups.

Tips for Enjoying Xi’an’s Nightlife

  • Dress Comfortably but Stylishly – Some clubs have dress codes.
  • Bring Cash – Smaller bars might not accept cards.
  • Stay Hydrated – Alternate drinks with water to avoid a rough morning.
  • Use Didi (China’s Uber) – Safe and cheap way to get back if you stay out late.

Beyond Pub Crawls: Other Nightlife Experiences in Xi’an

If you want to mix it up, here are more ways to enjoy Xi’an after dark:

Live Music Venues

  • Moonkey Bar – Great for indie bands.
  • Meet Tang Bar – A mix of jazz and electronic music.

Rooftop Bars

  • Flair Bar – Offers stunning views of the city skyline.

Late-Night Street Food

Don’t miss the Muslim Quarter’s night market for post-drinking snacks like yangrou paomo (lamb stew with bread).

Final Thoughts

Staying at a hostel with free pub crawls in Xi’an is a fantastic way to experience the city’s nightlife without breaking the bank. Whether you’re into craft beer, dancing, or just meeting new people, these hostels make it easy to have an unforgettable night out. So pack your bags, book a bunk, and get ready to explore Xi’an—one bar at a time!
