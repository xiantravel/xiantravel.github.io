Hostels in Xian with Bunk Beds & Privacy Curtains

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Xi’an, the ancient capital of China, is a must-visit destination for history buffs, food lovers, and backpackers alike. With its iconic Terracotta Warriors, vibrant Muslim Quarter, and stunning city walls, Xi’an offers an unforgettable travel experience. For budget-conscious travelers, hostels with bunk beds and privacy curtains are a game-changer—combining affordability, comfort, and a social atmosphere. Here’s a guide to the top hostels in Xi’an that offer these perks.

Why Choose a Hostel with Bunk Beds & Privacy Curtains?

Staying in a hostel doesn’t mean sacrificing comfort. Modern hostels in Xi’an have upgraded their dormitory setups to include:

  • Privacy curtains – Perfect for light sleepers or those who want a bit of personal space.
  • Comfortable bunk beds – Many now feature memory foam mattresses and individual reading lights.
  • Social vibes – Shared common areas make it easy to meet fellow travelers.
  • Budget-friendly prices – Often half the cost of a hotel room.

Whether you're a solo backpacker or a group of friends, these hostels provide the best of both worlds: affordability and a good night’s sleep.

Top Hostels in Xi’an with Bunk Beds & Privacy Curtains

1. Han Tang Inn International Hostel

Located just a short walk from the Bell Tower, Han Tang Inn is a favorite among backpackers.

Key Features:

  • Pod-style bunk beds with thick privacy curtains.
  • Free walking tours to nearby attractions.
  • Rooftop bar with stunning city views.
  • 24/7 reception for late check-ins.

Why Stay Here?

This hostel blends traditional Chinese décor with modern amenities. The staff are incredibly helpful, offering tips on the best local noodle spots and how to avoid tourist traps.

2. Xi’an Qixian Hostel

A hidden gem near the South Gate, Qixian Hostel is ideal for travelers who want a quieter stay.

Key Features:

  • Wooden bunk beds with blackout curtains.
  • Cozy courtyard for relaxing after a day of sightseeing.
  • Bike rentals available to explore the city walls.

Why Stay Here?

The atmosphere here is more intimate, making it great for digital nomads or those who prefer a laid-back vibe.

3. The Mix Hostel Xi’an

If you’re looking for a party-friendly hostel with privacy options, The Mix Hostel is the place to be.

Key Features:

  • Capsule-style beds with charging ports.
  • Live music nights and pub crawls.
  • Free dumpling-making classes.

Why Stay Here?

This hostel is perfect for social butterflies who still want their own space at night.

How to Pick the Right Hostel for Your Trip

Location Matters

  • Near the Bell Tower – Best for first-time visitors.
  • Close to the Train Station – Convenient for early departures.
  • In the Muslim Quarter – Ideal for foodies.

Amenities to Look For

  • Free Wi-Fi & Lockers – Essential for security and staying connected.
  • Common Kitchen – Save money by cooking your own meals.
  • Tour Desk – Book Terracotta Warriors tours directly at the hostel.

Insider Tips for Staying in Xi’an Hostels

  • Book in Advance – Popular hostels fill up quickly, especially during peak seasons.
  • Bring Earplugs – Even with privacy curtains, some dorms can be noisy.
  • Try the Local Breakfast – Many hostels serve jianbing (savory pancakes) or baozi (steamed buns).

Final Thoughts

Xi’an’s hostels have evolved to meet the needs of modern travelers, offering both affordability and comfort. Whether you prefer a social party hostel or a quiet retreat, there’s an option for every type of traveler. So pack your bags, book a bunk bed with a privacy curtain, and get ready to explore one of China’s most fascinating cities!
