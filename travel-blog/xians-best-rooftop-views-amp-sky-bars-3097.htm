Xian’s Best Rooftop Views & Sky Bars

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Xian, an ancient city steeped in history, is not just about terracotta warriors and ancient pagodas. Over the past decade, it has transformed into a modern metropolis with a thriving nightlife scene. Among its many attractions, rooftop bars and sky-high viewpoints have become a must-visit for travelers seeking breathtaking panoramas of the city’s skyline. Whether you’re sipping cocktails under the stars or capturing the perfect sunset shot, Xian’s elevated venues offer unforgettable experiences.

Why Xian’s Rooftop Scene Stands Out

Unlike other Chinese cities known for their towering skyscrapers, Xian’s charm lies in its seamless blend of old and new. From rooftop bars overlooking the illuminated Bell Tower to modern lounges with views of the ancient city walls, these spots provide a unique perspective on Xian’s duality.

The Magic of Sunset and Skyline Views

There’s something magical about watching the sun dip behind Xian’s historic landmarks while the city lights begin to twinkle. The contrast between ancient architecture and contemporary high-rises creates a visual feast that’s hard to find elsewhere.

Top Rooftop Bars in Xian

1. Flair Rooftop Bar at The Ritz-Carlton

Perched on the 38th floor, Flair offers one of the most luxurious rooftop experiences in Xian. With an extensive cocktail menu and a sleek, modern design, this bar is perfect for those who appreciate sophistication. The view of the Qujiang New District’s skyline is nothing short of spectacular.

Must-Try Drink: The "Terracotta Sour"

A creative twist on the classic whiskey sour, this cocktail pays homage to Xian’s most famous archaeological wonder with a hint of local spices.

2. Cloud Lounge at Sofitel Xian on Renmin Square

Located in the heart of the city, Cloud Lounge combines French elegance with Xian’s vibrant energy. The open-air terrace provides an unobstructed view of the Bell Tower, making it a prime spot for photography enthusiasts.

Best Time to Visit: Golden Hour

Arrive just before sunset to witness the Bell Tower bathed in golden light—a sight that’s even more enchanting when paired with a glass of champagne.

3. Xian Sky Bar at Gran Meliá

This stylish rooftop bar is known for its infinity pool and 360-degree views of the city. Whether you’re lounging by the water or enjoying tapas at the bar, the atmosphere here is effortlessly chic.

Pro Tip: Reserve a Poolside Cabana

For the ultimate VIP experience, book a cabana in advance and enjoy personalized service while taking in the panoramic vistas.

Hidden Gems for Rooftop Views

1. The Secret Garden at Huimin Street

Tucked above the bustling Muslim Quarter, this lesser-known rooftop café offers a peaceful escape from the crowds. Sip on traditional Chinese tea while gazing at the maze of historic rooftops below.

2. Moonlight Bar at Tang West Market

A favorite among locals, this intimate rooftop bar is perfect for those who prefer a low-key vibe. The retro-inspired décor and live acoustic music create a cozy atmosphere.

Rooftop Dining with a View

1. Nova Grill at Hyatt Regency Xian

For travelers who want to combine fine dining with stunning views, Nova Grill is the place to be. Their grilled specialties and extensive wine list make it a top choice for a romantic evening.

2. The Roof at Westin Xian

This all-day dining venue transforms into a stylish rooftop bar at night. The buffet spread is impressive, but the real draw is the unobstructed view of the ancient city walls.

Tips for Visiting Xian’s Rooftop Venues

  • Dress Code: Most upscale rooftop bars enforce a smart-casual dress code. Avoid flip-flops and tank tops if you’re heading to luxury spots like Flair or Cloud Lounge.
  • Reservations: Popular venues fill up quickly, especially on weekends. Book a table in advance to secure the best seats.
  • Weather Check: Xian’s weather can be unpredictable. Always check the forecast before planning a rooftop outing—opt for indoor lounges on rainy days.

Capturing the Perfect Rooftop Photo

Xian’s skyline is a photographer’s dream, but capturing it requires some skill. Here’s how to get the best shots:

Golden Hour Magic

The soft, warm light during sunset enhances the city’s textures and colors. Position yourself facing the Bell Tower or ancient walls for a dramatic backdrop.

Nighttime Brilliance

For dazzling night shots, use a tripod to avoid blur. Long exposure settings will help capture the city’s vibrant lights.

Framing the Shot

Incorporate elements like cocktail glasses or rooftop décor to add depth to your photos.

Final Thoughts on Xian’s Rooftop Culture

From luxurious high-rise bars to hidden terraces, Xian’s rooftop scene offers something for every traveler. Whether you’re a cocktail connoisseur, a photography buff, or simply someone who loves a good view, these elevated spots promise unforgettable memories. So next time you’re in Xian, don’t just explore its ancient streets—take your experience to new heights.
