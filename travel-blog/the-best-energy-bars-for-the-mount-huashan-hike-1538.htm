The Best Energy Bars for the Mount Huashan Hike

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Mount Huashan, one of China’s Five Great Mountains, is a bucket-list destination for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Known for its steep trails, narrow plank paths, and breathtaking views, this hike demands stamina, focus, and—most importantly—the right fuel. Energy bars are a hiker’s best friend, providing quick nutrition without weighing you down. But not all bars are created equal. Here’s a guide to the best energy bars to power your Mount Huashan adventure.

Why Energy Bars Are Essential for the Huashan Hike

Hiking Mount Huashan isn’t just a walk in the park. The trails are rugged, the ascents are steep, and the famous "Plank Walk in the Sky" requires nerves of steel. You’ll burn hundreds of calories, and carrying heavy snacks isn’t practical. Energy bars offer:

  • Quick calories for sustained energy
  • Lightweight convenience (no bulky packaging)
  • Balanced macros (carbs, protein, and fats) to keep you going

Key Features to Look For

When choosing energy bars for Huashan, prioritize:

  1. High calorie-to-weight ratio – Every gram counts in your backpack.
  2. Natural ingredients – Avoid bars loaded with artificial additives that can cause stomach issues.
  3. Long shelf life – No refrigeration needed.
  4. Easy to eat on the go – No messy wrappers or crumbling.

Top Energy Bars for the Huashan Hike

1. CLIF Bar – The Classic All-Rounder

Why it works:
- 250+ calories per bar
- Balanced 4:1 carb-to-protein ratio
- Comes in flavors like Chocolate Chip and Peanut Butter

Best for: Hikers who want a reliable, no-fuss option.

2. RXBAR – Clean Protein Power

Why it works:
- Made with simple ingredients (dates, egg whites, nuts)
- 12g of protein per bar for muscle recovery
- No added sugars

Best for: Those who prefer whole-food nutrition.

3. LÄRABAR – Vegan-Friendly & Light

Why it works:
- Only 2-7 ingredients per bar
- Gluten-free and dairy-free
- Sweetened naturally with dates

Best for: Hikers with dietary restrictions.

4. Kind Nut Bars – Crunchy & Satisfying

Why it works:
- Packed with nuts for healthy fats
- Lower sugar than many competitors
- Great texture (no sticky mess)

Best for: Snackers who love a bit of crunch.

5. ProBar Meal – A Full Mini-Meal

Why it works:
- 350-400 calories per bar
- Contains superfoods like chia and quinoa
- More filling than standard bars

Best for: Long hikes where you need substantial fuel.

Honorable Mentions

  • GoMacro Bars – Organic and plant-based
  • Honey Stinger Waffles – Quick-digesting carbs
  • GU Energy Chews – For an extra boost on steep sections

Pro Tips for Packing Energy Bars

  • Bring variety – Flavor fatigue is real after hours of hiking.
  • Pre-open wrappers – Saves time and avoids littering.
  • Pair with electrolytes – Hydration is just as important as calories.

Final Thoughts

The right energy bar can make or break your Huashan experience. Whether you need a protein-packed RXBAR or a calorie-dense ProBar, choose wisely and hike smart. The mountain’s challenges are legendary—fuel up and conquer them like a pro.
